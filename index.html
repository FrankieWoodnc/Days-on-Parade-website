ABOUT

Days On Parade are a neo-psychedelic punk band from St. Catharines. Their fast-paced, blend of turbocharged whimsical riffs, chunky modern energy, and hypnotizing grooves have made them a staple of the new wave of Niagara Alternative music. Days on Parade continue through their first years as a band on a hyperactive unrelenting momentum that keeps their growing audiences coming back for more. Their latest release, "Look Alive" (Recorded at Stonehouse and Suitcase Studios in St. Catharines, Ontario) dives into a journey of fierce and biting songwriting. Fueled by a relentless schedule of shows ever-expanding from Ontario, Days On Parade has shared stages with DZ Deathrays, Dead Tired, Protest The Hero, Single Mothers, and more. Days On Parade continues to push themselves forward with a DIY ethic and an undeniable knack for experimentation.
